Résumé

Hi guys! My name is Ryu Hoshi. You may know me from the game "Street Fighter". Yes, I am keen on martial arts. Blood and dead enemies make me satisfied. My credo is to live is to fight and to fight is to live. But don't panic! I am kind and gentle-hearted man. I respect others with skills equal to mine.
